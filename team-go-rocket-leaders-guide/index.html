Pokemon Go: Team GO Rocket Leaders Guide

November 10, 2019 Gaurav Yadav

Team GO Rocket Leaders have been appearing in Pokemon Go for a few days now. We have made a guide to help you find and defeat Team Rocket Leaders (Cliff, Sierra, and Arlo).

Steps to encounter a Leader:

  • Defeating a Team GO Rocket grunts awards you Mysterious Components.
  • By collecting 6 Mysterious Components, Rocket Detector can be formed.
  • With the help of Rocket Detector bring up the overworld map that will direct you to the Team GO Rocket boss.
  • Leaders use shields and Team Leader shadow Pokemon can be shiny.

Rewards

You get 1000 Stardust and two of:

  • Unova Stone
  • Sinnoh Stone
  • Revive
  • Max Revive
  • Max Potion
