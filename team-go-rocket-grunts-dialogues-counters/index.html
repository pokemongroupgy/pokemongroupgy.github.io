Team Go Rocket: Grunts Dialogues and Counters

August 4, 2019 Gaurav Yadav

Team Go Rocket battles can be quite difficult and tricky, so you need to have a team that has good counters to them. You can know what kind of Pokemon the Grunt will have from their dialogue.

Team Go Rocket randomly and independently selects one Pokemon from each of the lists below to build a team, which are sent in the same order.

Table of Contents
  • Team Go Rocket Dialogues
  • Grunts Counters
  • Team Go Rocket Leader Counters

Pokemon in Green Box has a chance of being captured after the battle.

Addition Info:

  • Grunts never use their Protect Sheild but leaders will always try to use their Protect Shields.
  • Pokemon dropped by leaders can be shiny.

The Team Go Rocket grunts dialogue indicates the strongest Pokemon, not the one you can catch.

Team Go Rocket Dialogues

Keep your eye in these dialogues:

  • NORMAL – Normal doesn’t mean weak.
  • FIGHTING – This buff physique isn’t just for show!
  • GROUND – You’ll be defeated into the ground!
  • FLYING – My bird Pokémon wants to battle with you!
  • POISON – Coiled and ready to strike!
  • ROCK – Let’s rock and roll!

  • BUG – Go, my super bug Pokémon!
  • FIRE – Do you know how hot Pokémon fire breath can get?
  • WATER – These waters are treacherous!
  • GRASS – Don’t tangle with us!
  • ELECTRIC – Get ready to be shocked!
  • GHOST – Ke…ke…ke…ke…ke…ke!
  • PSYCHIC – Are you scared of physics that use unseen power?
  • DRAGON – ROAR! … How’d that sound?

Random Dialogues:

  • Don’t bother, I’ve already won.
  • Get ready to be defeated!
  • Winning is for the winners.

  • DARK – Wherever is a light, there is also shadow.
  • FAIRY – Check out my cute Pokémon!
  • ICE – You’re gonna be frozen in your tracks.
  • STEEL – You’re no match for my iron will!
