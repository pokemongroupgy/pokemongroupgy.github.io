SUMMARY

Inur is a Arcana type Draconius GO creature with 1850 Max CP. Inur best moveset is Sphere of Peril with Ionization. Inur takes increased damage from Earth and reduced damage from Water type attacks. Inur evolves into Morgoroth for 150 stones.
