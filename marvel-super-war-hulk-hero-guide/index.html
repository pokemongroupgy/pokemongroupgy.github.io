Jade Giant

If there are no enemy heroes within 250 yards, the Hulk will throw an enormous rock in the specified direction, dealing 80(+50% Bonus Physical Attack) (+5% Target Max HP) physical damage to enemies in its path and slowing them by 30% for 1.5 seconds. If there are enemy heroes within 250 yards of the Hulk, he will grab and trash the one with the lowest HP three times, each time dealing 20(+5% Target Max HP) physical damage to the target, all the while being invincible.

Early Game Guide

Early game Hulk can be easily targeted from range due to his limited mobility, enemy will keep poking him from range. To counter that work on leveling up Jade Giant first as it will help you attack from distance.

Do not engage enemies alone until you reach level 5 or if you have clear HP advantage. Focus mainly on holding and clearing your lane. Keep attacking minions to level up fast as you won’t die easily even if the enemy hits you with an attack or two.

Start engaging the enemies after you have unlocked Hulk Ultimate Skill Shockwave.

Note: To escape adverse conditions, do not just run as Hulk is really slow and the enemy will catch him easily. To outrun an opponent or even enemy team, when the enemy catch you use hulk smash first then second time use Jade Giant. This will improve your chances of escaping.

Recommended Combo

Shokewave (Ult) – Jade Giant – Basic Attack – Hulk Smash – Basic Attack

This combo is for early game and it will help to kill enemies when fighting 1v1.

End Game Guide

Once Hulk reaches level 10 he would have gained enough HP and Armour to on any opponent Hero or maybe two at a time. If you are using a Hulk build we have suggested and have obtained Megingjord, he will keep healing himself when not in combat.

This healing will allow you to keep fighting with the need to report back home to heal.

If you have an assassin and energy hero in your team, ask them to stick by and your trio will become almost invincible.

Recommended Combo

Shokewave (Ult) – Hulk Smash – Basic Attack – Jade Giant  – basic Attack
