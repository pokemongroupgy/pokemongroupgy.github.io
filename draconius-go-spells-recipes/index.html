Draconius Go: Spells and Recipes

August 8, 2019 Gaurav Yadav

Recipes in Draconius Go are unlockable elements used to cast spells at Altar. Recipes are received by completing quests. Every Spell has a different recipe and Runes requirement.

How to cast a Spell?

  • Go to Magic Tab, Magic is unlocked at Level 7.
  • Install the Altar
  • Recipes have different Runes requirement if you have all the Runes to can cast that spell.

List of recipes

Here is a list of all currently known recipes :
