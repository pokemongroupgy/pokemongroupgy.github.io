Jurassic World Alive: Dracoceratops Dinosaur

July 23, 2019 Gaurav Yadav

Dracoceratops in Jurassic World Alive is a super-hybrid dinosaur with high damage due to its rampage and swipe in rampage coupled with stun. Its belongs to God tier as it can beat almost any other dinosaurs in the game.

Dracoceratops Hybrid Requirement

To create this hybrid 200 DNA is needed.

Dracorex Gen 2

  • Require: Level 15
  • Cost: 500x DNA

Triceratops Gen 2

  • Require: Level 15
  • Cost: 500x DNA

Battle Analysis

This super hybrid legendary dinosaur has high damage but slow speed. Its swipe in Rampage is something to be aware of, then it can also stun and deal another rampage.
