Battle Basics

Battles in Pokemon Go can only occur at a Gym. Whether you’re training at a friendly Gym or challenging an opposing team, you’ll need to know the basics.

Battles are in real time which demands you to act quickly. Simply tap the screen to attack with your Pokemon’s basic attack and swipe left or right to dodge before your opponent’s attack hits.

Once you’ve filled up some of the blue meter below your Pokemon’s health bar by attacking normally, you can unleash a more powerful attack by holding down on the screen before releasing.

Battle Wiki
