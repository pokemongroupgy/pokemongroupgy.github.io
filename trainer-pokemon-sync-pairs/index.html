Pokemon Masters: Trainer and Pokemon Sync Pairs (65)

July 25, 2019 Gaurav Yadav

The new Pokemon game Pokemon Masters takes place on the artificial island of Pasio. Trainers and their Pokemon on Pasio are called sync pairs and they share a special bond.

In Pokemon Masters, many famous Trainers and their Pokemon partners from every region has gathered on Pasio and have formed their own sync pairs.

Special outfits known as Syna Outfits can be used by trainers, which allows them to change their Sync Pair.

Sync Pairs also has a special role, which tells us their focus and how they should be used.

  • Strike – Focus on Attack/Special Attack with lower HP
  • Support – Increased HP and moves that boost their partners
  • Tech – Pokemon that typically afflict status

Trainers also have a set potential which determines their stats and the maximum level they can reach.
