Draconius Go Cost per Power Up: Essence and Stones

November 11, 2017 Gaurav Yadav

In Draconius Go a player can make their creature stronger by either powering it up or evolving it. Creatures have fixed evolution and they cost a fixed amount of stones to evolve, but powering up a creature cost stone plus essence.

Each power-up requires a different amount of essence and stones. So we have collected some data about the cost of Power Up for you to use and plan your power-ups accordingly.

Tip: Essence are stones are limited so be careful before spending them, at present we recommend you save your essence and stones and only think about evolving or powering up after you reach 21 level (higher the level better it will be for you). If you want to fight and win random encounters, just power up one of your creatures, which will be more than enough for winning random encounters.
