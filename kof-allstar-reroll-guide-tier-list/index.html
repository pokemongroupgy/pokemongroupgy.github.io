KOF ALLSTAR: Reroll Guide and Tier List

January 10, 2020 Gaurav Yadav

The King of Fighters ALLSTAR was released on global servers on October 22, 2019, by Netmarble. This Reroll Guide and Tier List will help you get a right start if you are planning to restart your account.

Reroll Guide

If you are downloading the game again then, do not download all, just medium and skip the tutorial.

General Reroll Guide

  • Select ticket 98 Kyo, 96 Ryo or 95 Yuri
  • Clear Chapter ’94 up to chapter 3 to unlock upgrades
  • Do rolls on the wheel to get red exp potions
  • Go to your fighter’s status menu
  • Throw EXP stuff at your carry
  • Unlock desperation skill and your third skill in Fighter Core Tree.
  • Keep auto-battling
  • Clear Chapter ’94 Chapter 8
  • Go to the home menu
  • Go to the secret shop
  • Summon for Orochi Iori
  • If Orochi Iori is not added, uninstall/reinstall or delete cache via root
  • Repeat as necessary

Tier Wise Reroll Guide

C-Tier Reroll

  1. Pull on any banner and just play the game.

B-Tier Reroll

  1. Pull on Infinity Banner, get King, Mai or Shermie. (seems like you can only get 1 5* from Infinity Banner.
  2. Get any other 5* from Selector Ticket
  3. Play the game

A-Tier Reroll

  1. Pull on Step-up Banner to get Kyo95 or any 2 5* character
  2. Get any other 5* from Selector Ticket
  3. Play the game

S-Tier Reroll

  1. Pull on Infinity Banner, get King, Mai or Shermie.
  2. Get any other 5* from Selector Ticket
  3. Play until chapter 8 to unlock Orochi banner
  4. Pull Orochi Iori or Orochi Leona
  5. Start the game

SS-Tier Reroll

  1. Get Shermie from Selector Ticket (She’s purple and useful for chapter 8 last boss)
  2. Play until chapter 8 to unlock Orochi banner
  3. Pull Orochi Iori or Orochi Leona (you will have enough for 2 tries)
  4. Start the game

SSS-Tier Reroll

  1. Pull on Step-up Banner to get Kyo95
  2. Get any other 5* from Selector Ticket
  3. Play until chapter 8 to unlock Orochi banner
  4. Pull Orochi Iori or Orochi Leona (you will have enough for 1 try). Play another 1 chapter for second try.
  5. Start the game
