Pokemon GO: Mew Quest Goals

April 3, 2018 Gaurav Yadav

Pokemon Go pushed the update where have players to go out and search for Mew. Loading screen with Professor Willow in desperate search of a Mew that’s floating right behind him was out even before that.

This update is one of the largest updates to the game as it brings new “Research” system. Players complete daily quests for prizes including rare items and even legendary Pokemon encounters.

A “Special Research” has also been introduced in which players have to hunt down mythical Pokemon Mew.

[adinserter block=”1″]

While Field Research tasks are randomized, Mew quest will follow a more specific path.

Step 1
  • Spin 5 Pokestops
  • Catch 10 Pokemon
  • Transfer 5 Pokemon.

Step 2: Start walking!

  • Earn 2 candies with your buddy
  • Make 10 Great Throws
  • Hatch 3 eggs

These are the first 2 step total 8 steps which will become tougher as you move forward. Some data has been mined as to what others tasks can be included in the steps to follow

Datamined quest information:

  • Battling Pokemon
  • Evolving Pokemon
  • Catching specific ones like Ditto

This is…pretty exciting! We know a lot about Mew already from the data in the game like it has a ton of different moves it can learn from all different types. Its max CP should be just over 3,000 and it’s supposed to have a 100% catch rate, so at the end of this quest, you won’t have to worry about it going away.

Get out there and start completing objectives.
