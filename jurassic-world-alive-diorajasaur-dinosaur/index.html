Jurassic World Alive: Diorajasaur Dinosaur

July 23, 2019 Gaurav Yadav

Diorajasaur in Jurassic World Alive is a super-hybrid dinosaur with ferocity coupled with a precise counter-attack, distraction, invincibility, and distraction cleanse. Its belongs to God tier as it can beat almost any other dinosaurs in the game.

Diorajasaur Hybrid Requirement

To create this hybrid 250 DNA is needed.

Rajakylosaurus

  • Require: Level 20
  • Cost: 50x DNA

Tuojiangosaurus

  • Require: Level 20
  • Cost: 500x DNA

Diorajasaur Moves

Superiority Strike

  • Cleanse distraction
  • Deal 1x damage
  • Target’s speed decreases by 50% for 1 turn

Ferocious Strike

  • Deal 1x damage
  • Damage increased by 50% for 3 turns.
  • Cooldown: 3

Instant Distraction

  • Priority.
  • Target’s damage is reduced by 90% for 1 turn.
  • Cooldown: 1

Instant Invincibility

  • Priority.
  • Shield 100% for 1 turn.
  • Cooldown: 3
  • Delay: 1

Passive Ability

Precise Shattering Counter

  • Destroy Shields.
  • Deal 1x Armor Piercing damage while ignoring Cloak and Evasive effects after receiving and surviving a physical attack.

Battle Analysis

This super hybrid unique dinosaur has high HP and an Armor but slow speed. Its Ferocius move with counter deals high damage and its distraction, invincibility allows it to take damage and fight for long.
