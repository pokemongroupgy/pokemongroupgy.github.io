Why is Max CP important?

Max CP is an indication of Creatures power and strength in Draconius Go. But CP is not the only stat that matters, HP determines how much damage a creature will be able to take. CP and HP seem to be independent of each other, unlike Pokemon Go.

We will soon be bringing you the Max Hp chart, as Max CP and Max Hp are needed to determine the which creatures are best in the game.
