Jurassic World Alive: Erlidominus Dinosaurs

June 24, 2018 Gaurav Yadav

Erlidominus is hybrid with Unique rarety. It is one of the strongest dinosaurs in Jurassic World Alive. It is a genetically modified hybrid, created by the fusion of Erlikosaurus and Indominus Rex.

Table of Contents

  1. Hybrid Cost Analysis
  2. Erlidominus Stats
  3. Moves
  4. Erlidominus Level Stats

Erlidominus is classified as Unique and costs 50 DNA of Indominus Rex and 200 DNA of Erlikosaurus, plus 1000 coins to create a random number of DNA strands (anything from 10 to 90). 250 DNA is needed to create this hybrid dino. Both Erlikosaurus and Indominus Rex should be level 20 in order to fuse and create the hybrid.
